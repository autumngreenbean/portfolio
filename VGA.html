ANALOG CLOCK AUDIOVISUAL SYNTHESIZER (VGA)

Summary: For this project, I designed and fabricated a VGA connector to interface with a dual 1/4" TS (Tip-Sleeve) audio cable, adapted for stereo audio input.

Specifications:: Injecting audio signals into the H-sync and V-sync channels of the custom VGA connector introduced signal interference. This allowed the audio input to modulate the timing of the sync signals, resulting in visual distortions and manipulations on the connected VGA display.

An Arduino Nano was used to control the pixel array and manage the animations displayed on the screen.

I integrated several switches and buttons from a harvested analog clock. These inputs were used to toggle between different visual modes and effects, as demonstrated in video #4.
