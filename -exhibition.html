What is a Feedback Loop?

A 'feedback loop' is a term used in video synthesis.

This technique involves taking the output of a video signal or a portion of it and routing it back into the input of the same video signal or processing chain.

This can result in a variety of visually intriguing and often abstract effects, including patterns, distortions, and complex visual compositions.


In my process, I utilize a procedure of methods to create visualizations using a VHS camcorder and a second monitor.

Through active experimentation, I have been able to achieve various different effects and visualizations ranging from pure abstractions to tangible representations.
