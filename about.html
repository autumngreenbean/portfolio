About Autumn

Autumn Jing Green specializes in creating abstract and experimental visualizations using analog video synthesis and AI generated art to generate unique works of art that challenges traditional artistic expression.

Their analog video synthesis technique involves using electronic signals to create unique and unpredictable visual effects. By manipulating various signal parameters, a wide range of images and patterns are generated.

AI generated art via stable diffusion involves using algorithims to generated abstract images. The technique utilizes the principles of diffusion to create unique and intricate patterns that are impossible to replicate by hand.


Both these processes are intended to push the boundaries between art and technology.

By pushing the limits of these technologies, others can be encouraged to embrace new mediums and techniques to challenge themselves to think beyond traditional artistic expression.
